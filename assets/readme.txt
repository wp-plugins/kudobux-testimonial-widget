== Description ==

Kudobux Testimonial Widget plugin allows you to display social testimonials  or selected quotes on your WordPress blog. 
Kudobux grabs all the positive and great things people are saying about you on social media(Facebook,Twitter,Blogs...),
filters them and display it on your website with an easy to customize clean widget. 
With Kudobux you always get upto date testimonials on your site, this will wow your web visitors and improve your SEO drastically. 
It is easy to setup and 100% free.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `Kudobux.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Visit the Kudobux Menu and start to customize your Kudos
